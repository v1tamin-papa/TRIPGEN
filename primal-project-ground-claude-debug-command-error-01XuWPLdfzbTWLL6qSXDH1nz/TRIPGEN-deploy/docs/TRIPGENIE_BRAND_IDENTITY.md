## 3. TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM

### Primary Font (Headlines)

**Font**: Inter
**Backups**: SF Pro Display, -apple-system, Helvetica Neue, sans-serif

**Character**: Modern, clean, highly legible, tech-forward yet friendly

**Weights Used**:
- Extra Bold (800) ‚Äî Main headlines, hero text
- Bold (700) ‚Äî Section headers, card titles
- Semi Bold (600) ‚Äî Subheaders, emphasis

**Usage**:
- Hero headlines: 48-64px, Extra Bold
- Section titles: 32-48px, Bold
- Card headers: 18-24px, Semi Bold

---

### Secondary Font (Body)

**Font**: Inter
**Backups**: SF Pro Text, -apple-system, Helvetica Neue, sans-serif

**Character**: Highly readable, friendly, clean

**Weights Used**:
- Regular (400) ‚Äî Body text, descriptions
- Medium (500) ‚Äî UI elements, buttons, labels

**Usage**:
- Body text: 16-18px, Regular, 1.6 line height
- UI labels: 14px, Medium
- Small text: 12-14px, Regular

**Note**: Using Inter for both headers and body creates consistency while weight variations provide hierarchy. Inter is free, widely supported, and optimized for screens.

---

### Alternative Premium Option

**Headlines**: Satoshi or General Sans
**Body**: Inter

This combination adds more personality to headlines while maintaining excellent body readability.

## 4. BRAND VOICE & PERSONALITY

### Brand Personality (5 Adjectives)

#### 1. Intelligent
Shows up in: Anticipating user needs, smart recommendations, learning preferences over time. Copy that demonstrates understanding: "We noticed you prefer boutique hotels..."

#### 2. Effortless
Shows up in: Simple language, minimal steps to achieve goals, "it just works" feeling. Never making travel planning feel like work.

#### 3. Delightful
Shows up in: Moments of surprise, helpful discoveries, celebrating bookings. Small touches that make users smile. Using "magic" language appropriately.

#### 4. Trustworthy
Shows up in: Clear pricing, no hidden fees, honest recommendations, data privacy messaging. Professional tone when discussing money and bookings.

#### 5. Personal
Shows up in: Remembering preferences, addressing users by name, feeling like a friend who knows travel. Not corporate or robotic.

---

### Tone of Voice Spectrum

#### Formal vs Casual: **60% Casual**
We're friendly and approachable, like a knowledgeable friend who happens to be amazing at travel planning. We avoid stiff corporate language but maintain professionalism when discussing bookings, payments, and commitments.

**Do**: "Let's find you the perfect spot"
**Don't**: "Please proceed to select your accommodation preferences"

#### Technical vs Simple: **80% Simple**
We hide complexity and speak in plain language. Users don't need to know how our AI works ‚Äî they just need to know it works. Technical terms only when necessary (flight numbers, confirmation codes).

**Do**: "We found a better price for your flight"
**Don't**: "Our algorithm detected a fare class adjustment opportunity"

#### Playful vs Serious: **55% Playful**
We have personality and aren't afraid to use light humor or travel puns occasionally. But we stay grounded when it matters ‚Äî booking confirmations, payment processing, and problem resolution are straightforward and clear.

**Do**: "Your Chicago food adventure is ready! üçï"
**Don't**: "LOL ur trip is gonna be SO fire fam"

## 6. VISUAL STYLE GUIDELINES

### Photography Style

**Subject Matter**:
- Real travelers (not stock-looking models) enjoying experiences
- Authentic destination shots: local restaurants, boutique hotels, hidden gems
- Devices showing the app in real-world contexts (airport, caf√©, hotel)
- Food, architecture, nature ‚Äî things travelers actually photograph

**Mood**:
- Warm, inviting, aspirational but achievable
- Natural lighting, golden hour preferred
- Candid over posed
- Joy and discovery, not luxury flexing

**Color Treatment**:
- Warm color grading, slightly saturated
- Lift shadows slightly for an airy feel
- Consistent warm white balance
- Occasional coral/orange color overlays for brand consistency

**Avoid**:
- Cold, corporate stock photos
- Overly filtered Instagram aesthetic
- Crowded tourist spots
- Stressed or overwhelmed travelers

---

### Graphic Elements

**Shape Language**:
- Rounded corners (12-24px radius) ‚Äî friendly, approachable
- Soft shadows ‚Äî depth without harshness
- Organic curves balanced with geometric structure
- No sharp corners or aggressive angles

**Pattern Usage**:
- Subtle dot grids for backgrounds
- Topographic/map-inspired line patterns for travel context
- Gradient mesh backgrounds for hero sections
- Use sparingly ‚Äî patterns support, never dominate

**Icon Style**:
- Outline style with 2px stroke weight
- Rounded caps and joins
- Consistent 24x24px grid
- Single color (adapt to context)
- Friendly and recognizable at small sizes

---

### UI Component Style

**Buttons**:
- Primary: Coral gradient (#FF6B6B ‚Üí #FF8E53), white text, 12px radius, subtle shadow
- Secondary: White/transparent, coral border, coral text
- Hover: Slight lift (-2px translateY), enhanced shadow

**Cards**:
- White background, subtle border or shadow
- 16-20px radius
- Generous padding (24-32px)
- Hover: lift effect, border color change

**Input Fields**:
- Light gray background (#F8F9FA)
- 8px radius
- Coral focus ring
- Placeholder text in muted gray
